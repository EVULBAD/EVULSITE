3D COMMISSION INFO

for 2D commissions, head this direction

3D commissions are open!
2 slots available

every model comes fully textured and rigged. every posed render comes with a simple gradient background. additional versions of said render (i.e. transparent or outlined) can be made upon request.

"base models", which only apply if your character normally wears full outfits, are an additional version of your model that is unclothed. this makes it easier for me or other 3D modellers to create additional outfits for your model in the future (or, y'know, if you just happen to want an unclothed version of your character for... whatever reason).

every add-on/upgrade can be done retrospectively, so don't worry if you decide you want something done after you've already gotten a model from me. just fill out the order form again and state in the "additional info" section that i've already made you a model and you're looking to add on to it.

complicated designs may cost extra depending on complexity. some designs may even get rejected due to the fact that i may not feel confident in modelling them. modelling is hard work and i want to be sure i can make you something i'm proud of!

if you're unsure about whether or not i'm willing to model something, just ask. i'm open minded, dignity is a social construct, and the worst i'll do is say no.

rush orders are not available for 3D commissions at this time.
