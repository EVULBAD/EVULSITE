PROGESS
While turn-around is heavily dependent on queue, complexity of commissions in said queue, and my life outside of art, commissions may take up to 5 months to complete once started. By default, I provide only one progress update upon finishing the sketch. You may request more progress updates at any point in time, however, I do not work on commissions every single day, and cannot guarantee significant day-to-day progress.

PAYMENT AND REFUNDS
All payment is upfront and full through Square. I will not start a commission until full payment is received. There are no rain-checks; if you have purchased a commission from me in the past at a cheaper price and my prices have since risen, you will not get any new commissions at those old prices.
Full refunds as requested by purchaser are only permitted under the following simultaneous conditions:
1. It has been 3 months since payment was received.
2. There has not been any progress upon commission in that time.
If your commission is a rush order that is not finished within the promised 2 week timespan, I will refund the rush order fee and offer a partial refund for whatever work I have not yet completed.

EDITS
Once commission is finished, I will provide only one major edit when requested. Edits will only be provided if requested up to a week afterward. Any edits requested after commission has been finished for one week may be provided alongside additional fees.
